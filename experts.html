Caitlyn Sharpshooting Tip's

"Wait for your moment." -- Being patient is more important than a desultry shot.

"A well-placed shot can save more than just time." -- Accuracy and position not speed.

"Know your target—before you take the shot." -- Know thy enemy.

"Your weapon is an extension of yourself." -- Know its ins and outs.

"Stay calm, even when things get heated." -- Don't panic and find your center.

"Predict the path, don’t just react." -- Predict future movements.

"Every shot teaches you something." -- Learn from your mistakes.

"Sometimes waiting is the best option." -- Know when to take the shot or not.

"The best defense is often a good offense." Keep your distance and control the fight.

"Being predictable is the first step to being caught off guard." -- Stay unpredicatable.

Ellie's Survival Tip's

"Don't make noise unless you have to." -- Stay quiet, stay hidden.

"The world’s not what it used to be, so you have to think on your feet." -- Adapt to your environment.

"You can’t always run away. Know when to fight and when to hide." -- Learn how to defend yourself.

"Trust your gut, but don’t let it get you killed." -- Know which instincts are wise to take.

"Sometimes, you have to make hard choices." -- Be prepared to sacrifice.

"Never let your guard down." -- Your weapon should always be ready.

"It’s a lot easier to survive with someone you trust." -- Be mindful of the allies you choose.

"This world will break you if you let it." -- Take care of your mental health.

"Sometimes, survival is all you’ve got left." -- Never lose hope, keep fighting.

Rick's Survival Tip's

"Even in the darkest of days, you have to find something worth living for." -- Don't forget to live.

"Relying on others too much can get you killed. Learn how to take care of yourself." -- Be self sufficient.

"You’ll lose people. Don’t let it break you." -- Remember who you are and separate it from those you lose.

"Nothing goes to waste in this world." -- Waste not want not.

"Hope is important, but you have to face the reality of what’s happening." -- Don't sugar coat it.

"There are times when walking away is the best choice." -- Fighting isn't always the answer.

"Family comes first, no matter the cost." -- Protect those you care about.

"If you stay still, you’ll get surrounded." -- Moving keeps you alive and agile.

"A leader has to make tough choices, even if they break your heart." -- Sacrifice is learned.

"You can’t trust everyone. People will let you down." -- Trust is earned, not given.

"In a crisis, don’t lose your head." -- Don't go seeing red.

Sometimes you have to be the one to stand up and help yourself.
